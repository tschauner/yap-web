SHORT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Yap is the only productivity app that guilt-trips, roasts, and nags you through push notifications - starring 12 AI agents including your mom, your ex, and your drill sergeant.


================================================================================
FULL DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Hi sweetie. It's Mom.
I noticed you still haven't done the thing. So I got you an app. You're welcome.

You tell us what you've been avoiding. You pick an agent. You set a deadline. Then we nag you until it's done. Not the "gentle reminder" kind. The kind where I question every decision I made as a parent.

There's 12 of us. I do guilt. The Therapist does that thing where he asks "what's really stopping you?" and then just waits. The Ex is here too - don't make eye contact. They'll say "typical" and somehow that works better than all of us combined. We don't talk about why.

Every message is AI-generated. Never the same twice. You can even tell us your weaknesses and we'll use them against you. Some agents remember your past missions. They keep receipts.

Anyway. You scrolled this far. You're not going to just close this and do nothing. Right?

Download it. For me. Please. I worry.

xo mom


================================================================================
AGENTS
================================================================================

FREE TIER (6 base agents):
  - Mom             Guilt trips that get personal fast
  - Best Friend     Bro energy, screenshots your failures
  - Boss            Corporate disappointment, inbox-style pressure
  - Drill Sergeant  All-caps military yelling
  - Therapist       "What's really stopping you?" ...then waits
  - Grandma         Survived a war. You can't finish one task.

PRO (6 special agents):
  - The Ex          Passive-aggressive, devastatingly specific
  - The Theorist    Convinced the system is designed to make you fail
  - The Colleague   Office-passive-aggressive, CC's everyone
  - Chef            Kitchen nightmare energy, your task is RAW
  - Disappointed Dad  Not angry. Just disappointed.
  - Gym Bro         Treats your task like leg day


================================================================================
KEY FEATURES
================================================================================

- 12 AI agents with distinct personalities and escalation styles
- AI-generated push notifications (GPT-4o) - never the same twice
- Notifications escalate the longer you procrastinate
- Custom Roast: share personal weaknesses, agents use them against you
- Agent Memory: agents remember your past failures (Pro)
- Global leaderboard ranking agents by real completion rates
- Quiet hours with customizable schedule
- Fully localized in 6 languages with culturally adapted humor
- One-time Pro purchase (no subscription)

================================================================================
TECHNICAL
================================================================================

- Built with Swift / SwiftUI
- Backend: Supabase (Edge Functions, PostgreSQL)
- AI: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for notification generation
- Push delivery: Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)
- Sound: Custom notification sounds via ElevenLabs
- Privacy-first: no email, name, or personal info required
  (device-level anonymous ID stored in Keychain)
- Optional Sign in with Apple for cross-device sync
